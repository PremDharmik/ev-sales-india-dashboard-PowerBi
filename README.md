# Electric Vehicle Sales in India – Power BI Dashboard

## 📌 Project Overview

This Power BI dashboard transforms raw, web-scraped data from the **Clean Mobility Shift** portal into a clean, analysis‑ready resource on electric vehicle (EV) adoption across India.  
The project involved rigorous data cleaning (removing null values and structural inconsistencies) to ensure high data integrity. The final dashboard provides a multi‑dimensional view of EV sales performance, market trends, and regional distribution – supporting strategic decisions in infrastructure planning, policy development, and market analysis.

## 📈 Major Insights from the Dashboard

### 1. Market Trajectory
- The **annual sales line chart** shows a clear transition from early adoption to mass‑market growth over the past decade.
- Monthly sales analysis reveals recurring **seasonality** (festivals, policy announcements, fiscal year cycles).

### 2. Vehicle Category Dominance
- **2‑wheelers (M‑Cycles/Scooters)** drive the majority of national volume.
- **3‑wheelers** are the second largest segment.
- A **funnel chart** visually confirms the steep drop‑off from high‑volume 2‑wheelers to niche segments (commercial, agricultural vehicles).

### 3. Geographic Distribution
- **Uttar Pradesh** and **Maharashtra** are the undisputed regional leaders.
- The **choropleth map** clearly visualises sales concentration.
- Leading states also support a **wider variety of vehicle models**, indicating a more mature and competitive EV ecosystem.

### 4. Strategic Recommendations
- Prioritise infrastructure investment in high‑volume states (UP, Maharashtra).
- Match investments with **targeted model launches** in diverse regional markets.
- Monitor emerging niche segments (commercial & agricultural EVs) for early‑stage opportunities.

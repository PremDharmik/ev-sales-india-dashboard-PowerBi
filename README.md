# Electric Vehicle Sales Dashboard – India
> Interactive Power BI dashboard for analyzing EV adoption trends across India

## 📖 Overview
This project focuses on transforming raw, web-scraped data from the Clean Mobility Shift portal into a clean, structured, and analysis-ready dataset. The primary goal was to enable reliable insights into electric vehicle (EV) adoption trends across India by ensuring high data integrity through rigorous preprocessing and cleaning.

The resulting Power BI dashboard provides a comprehensive, multi-dimensional view of India’s EV landscape. It highlights key metrics such as total sales (4M) and year-over-year growth (1.36%), enabling stakeholders to quickly assess market health and momentum.

Designed for analysts, policymakers, and business strategists, this project supports data-driven decision-making in areas such as market expansion, infrastructure planning, and policy development within the rapidly evolving EV ecosystem.

## ✨ Features
- 📊 KPI dashboard with total EV sales and YoY growth  
- 📈 Year-wise sales trend visualization  
- 🗺️ State-level EV adoption analysis (choropleth map)  
- 🚗 Vehicle category and class breakdown  
- 📉 Market share analysis by vehicle type  
- 🔍 Identification of high-growth segments and niches  
- 📅 Monthly and yearly trend analysis for seasonality insights  
- 📦 Strategic insights like portfolio optimization and segment performance  

## 🛠️ Tech Stack
- `Power BI` – Data visualization and dashboarding  
- `Data Cleaning & Preprocessing` – Handling null values and inconsistencies  
- `Web Scraping (Source Data)` – Clean Mobility Shift portal  
- `Data Analysis` – Trend and market analysis techniques

## 📊 How It Works
1. **Data Acquisition**  
   Raw EV data is collected from the Clean Mobility Shift portal.  

2. **Data Preparation**  
   - Removal of null values  
   - Fixing structural inconsistencies  
   - Ensuring clean, reliable dataset  

3. **Data Modeling & Visualization**  
   - KPI cards summarize market performance  
   - Line charts track growth trends  
   - Bar and pie charts show category dominance  
   - Choropleth maps visualize state-level distribution  

4. **Insights Generation**  
   - Identification of leading states (e.g., Uttar Pradesh, Maharashtra)  
   - Dominance of 2-wheelers and 3-wheelers  
   - Seasonal sales patterns  
   - Segment-level performance differences
